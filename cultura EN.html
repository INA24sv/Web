Who Are We?

About Us

We are a secondary education institution with over 40 years of training quality high school graduates, committed to society.

Mission

We are a secondary education institution that provides quality services, utilizing appropriate technological resources for the comprehensive training of competent individuals with a business vision, with the effective participation of the various actors and managers of the educational process.

Vision

To be a secondary education institution, a leader in the formation of comprehensive, competent individuals capable of developing in family, social, and higher education contexts, with a business projection.

Values

Quality in Education - Academic Excellence - Continuous Innovation - Social Commitment - Respect
